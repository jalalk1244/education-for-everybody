About Us:


Education for everybody is a politically unaffiliated, humanitarian aid organisation, whose goal is to make it possible for everybody to utilise their right for education. One of our core principles has been, since 2022, that the donation of people goes 100% unabridged to our project's goal. Our aim, in particular, is to help children in developing countries such as Afghanistan, Somalia, Mali and more to use their rights.


>We regard ourselves as a trustworthy intermediary between donors of donations and the children in need of them. The tasks are carried out by our volunteers who operate both abroad and in Sweden. Videos and photographs are used to document the project's progression. In our newsletter, you may learn more about them and stay updated.


We make sure that whether it comes to building projects or the acquisition and distribution of educational supplies, our partners choose regional entrepreneurs. By doing this, the money stays in the country, aiding in its long-term growth.
