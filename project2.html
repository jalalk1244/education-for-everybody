Fund a child in Somalia

Somalia is a country with on of the lowest literacy rate and 7 out of 10 Somalis are considered to be poor. This is why going to school and getting quality education are still distant dreams for many Somalian children. Through this project, you will be able to help children who otherwise couldn't afford to attend school by providing a year's worth of funding for their education. It costs 55$ monthly payment or 600$ one time payment to compensate a childs one year educational.
