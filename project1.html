Build schools in Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a country which has been in war conflicts for over 40 years. These conflicts have led to the destruction of infrastructure and educational centres such as schools. According to an estimate, nearly half of all school-aged children in Afghanistan are not attending school. A whole generation of children's hopes and dreams are being crushed. Building schools, in our opinion, is a good first step toward providing Afghanistan with high-quality education. This project's objective is to rebuild the ruined school buildings and build new ones to give the children a chance for education. Even one dollar could make a difference.
